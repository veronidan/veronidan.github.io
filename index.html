Older Buildings Are More Energy-efficient than Some Newer Buildings

Older Buildings with Good EnergyStar Ratings

The Chrysler Building in New York has an EnergyStar rating of 84/100. A score of at least 75 is required for a building to be considered "high efficiency".

Newer Buildings with Poor Energy Ratings

The Seagram Building in New York has an EnergyStar rating of 3/100.
